# Contributing to SaddleRAG

Thank you for your interest in contributing to SaddleRAG.

## License of Your Contribution

SaddleRAG is dual-licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License version 3 or later (AGPLv3) and a separate commercial license. By contributing, you agree that your contribution will be available under both licenses.

To make this legally clean, all contributors must sign the SaddleRAG Individual Contributor License Agreement (CLA) before their contributions can be merged. The CLA grants the project maintainer the right to license your contributions under both AGPLv3 and commercial terms.

## How to Contribute

1. Read the [Contributor License Agreement](./CLA.md).
2. Open a pull request with your proposed change.
3. In a comment on your pull request, include the following acknowledgment:

   > I have read and agree to the SaddleRAG Individual Contributor License Agreement.
   > Name: [Your full legal name]
   > Email: [Your email address]
   > Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]

4. The maintainer will review your change. Pull requests will not be merged until a valid CLA acknowledgment is on file.

## Bug Reports and Feature Requests

The GitHub issue tracker is for bug reports and feature requests. Commercial support questions are not handled via issues; commercial customers should email [support@jackalopetechnologies.com](mailto:support@jackalopetechnologies.com).
